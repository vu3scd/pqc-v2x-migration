# Future Work

This repository represents an initial structured approach. The following areas require further research and validation:

* PQC-optimized certificate formats for IEEE 1609.2 / ETSI TS 103 097
* Performance benchmarking of PQC algorithms on automotive-grade hardware
* Hybrid trust models for cross-border V2X deployments
* Privacy-preserving identity models under PQC constraints
* Integration of PQC into EV charging ecosystems (ISO 15118, OCPP)
* Hardware acceleration roadmap for PQC in OBUs and RSUs

Contributions, discussions, and industry feedback are encouraged.
